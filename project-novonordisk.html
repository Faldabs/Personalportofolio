3D

Three-dimentional (3D) refers to objects or environments that have three primary dimension: length,width, and height. In a 3D space, these dimension allow for depth perception and ther representation of physical volume. in computer graphics, 3D modeling involves creating virtual representation of objects with these three dimensions.

An unobstrusive approach

Consider the spatial arrangement of your 3D display. Whether it's a physical installation or a digital rendering, optimize the layout for maximum visibility and impact. This could include consideration for angles, perspectives and viewing distance

Creating a 3D display for Diabetic Day is not just about visual aesthetics; it's about harnessing the power of design to educate, inspire and raise awareness about an important health cause.
